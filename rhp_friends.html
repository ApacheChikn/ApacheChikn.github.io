To Some of the Most Exceptional People I Know,

When I was accepted into the Road Home Program, I saw it as a blessing. I expected to learn skills that would benefit me for life, moving beyond relying solely on medication. That was the extent of my expectations.

What I didn't expect was meeting such incredible people. I didn't expect to enjoy Chicago as much as I did. Most importantly, I didn't expect to form such meaningful bonds with people who truly understand me and want to stay connected even after the program ended.

You made me feel included, valued, and special. For that, I am deeply grateful. You are appreciated!

I love you all. ❤️❤️❤️
